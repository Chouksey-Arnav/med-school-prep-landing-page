Why doesn't Complex II pump protons in the ETC?
M
Complex II (Succinate dehydrogenase) is a peripheral membrane protein. It transfers electrons from FADH2 to Coenzyme Q, but it doesn't span the entire mitochondrial membrane, so it physically cannot pump protons into the intermembrane space.
